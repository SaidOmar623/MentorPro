About the Job

Responsibilities include working with a Deal internal development team. As a QA Analyst, you will work as part of a product development team testing custom built software solutions.

  • Create and run functional, application, regression, ad-hoc, and integration tests; including both positive and negative scenarios

  • Follow standard QA methodologies to ensure the delivery of quality products.

  • Upload test evidences in Jira

  • Log and report defects in Jira and escalate issues

  • Create test case artifacts

  • Participate in an Agile development environment / Scrum team

  • Work to meet project schedules, timelines and deadlines

  • Provide accurate estimates to assigned tasks

  • Contribute ideas to project planning
